# CUJ: Proactive Insights
Date: 2026-03-20

## 1. Scenario
The CEO logs into the One Human Corp Agentic OS after returning from lunch. They want a quick overview of what the company has accomplished and where their attention is urgently needed.

## 2. Before Journey (The Problem)
- The CEO has to manually click through the "Cost", "Handoffs", and "Agents" tabs.
- They must mentally calculate if they are overspending on inactive SWE agents.
- They might miss a critical pending handoff from a Product Manager agent, delaying a feature launch by hours.
- This creates "dashboard noise" and operational friction, requiring the CEO to search for insights rather than receiving them.

## 3. After Journey (The Solution)
- The CEO logs into the platform and lands on the **Overview** page.
- Right below the main KPI row, a beautifully rendered **"Proactive Insights"** glassmorphic widget immediately catches their eye.
- The widget displays three distinct, actionable alerts:
  1.  **Efficiency Alert (Green):** "Support agent support-1 has been idle for 2 hours. Scale down to save $300/mo."
  2.  **Bottleneck Warning (Orange):** "3 pending handoffs from Product Managers. Pipeline velocity is degrading."
  3.  **Market Pulse (Blue):** "Growth Agent detected a 14% uptick in competitor keyword mentions."
- The CEO clicks on the pending handoffs alert and is instantly routed to the Handoffs page, allowing them to provide direction and unblock the agents.
- The CEO effortlessly manages the enterprise using AI that proactively pushes relevant insights.
